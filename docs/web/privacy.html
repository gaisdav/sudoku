Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Sudoku mobile application (“App”) for Android and iOS.

1. Data we collect

The App may collect or process the following:

  • Advertising data. The App uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). AdMob may collect device identifiers, advertising ID, and usage data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy. This is used to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.

2. How we use data

We use data only to operate the App and to serve ads. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third parties

Advertising is provided by Google. Their privacy policy and ad settings apply: Google Privacy Policy, Google Ad Settings.

4. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

6. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue or contact the maintainer via the App’s repository or distribution channel.
